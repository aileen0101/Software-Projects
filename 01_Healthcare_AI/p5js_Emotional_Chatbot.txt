p5.js Emotional Chatbot

Overview
--------
An LLM-powered chatbot that guides a novice programmer through translating a personal emotional experience — such as feeling like an outsider, feeling lonely in a crowd, or the elation of being on top of the world — into a creative p5.js sketch. The chatbot doesn't write the sketch for you; it helps you code and refine your own.

Task
----
Design and implement an LLM-powered chatbot that helps a novice programmer create p5.js sketches that creatively communicate a unique life experience of theirs.

Deliverable: a p5.js sketch that communicates what it felt like to be an outsider, produced in collaboration with the chatbot.

Why This Matters
----------------
This project mirrors a real challenge I care deeply about in AI for healthcare and beyond: designing AI systems that *enhance* human creativity, agency, and well-being rather than automate them away. In a clinical context, the same principle applies — AI should support patients and clinicians in expressing, understanding, and acting on their own experiences, not replace their judgment.

For me, this is an early exploration of how conversational AI can scaffold reflection and expression — patterns I want to carry into future healthcare ML work around mental health, patient journaling, and emotionally-aware care tools.
